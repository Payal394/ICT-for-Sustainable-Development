What is Sustainable Development?

Sustainable development is a concept that aims to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It is a balance between economic growth, social development, and environmental protection. The three dimensions of sustainable development are:



Economic: The economic dimension of sustainable development focuses on promoting economic growth that is sustainable over the long term. This means creating an economy that is resilient, diverse, and able to adapt to changing circumstances. Economic sustainability involves ensuring that resources are used efficiently, promoting innovation and technology, and creating opportunities for decent work and economic growth.

Social: The social dimension of sustainable development focuses on promoting social development that is equitable and inclusive. This means creating a society that is free from discrimination, promotes human rights, and provides access to basic services such as healthcare, education, and housing. Social sustainability involves promoting social cohesion, addressing inequality and poverty, and ensuring that everyone has a voice in decisions that affect their lives.

Environmental: The environmental dimension of sustainable development focuses on promoting environmental protection and conservation. This means preserving natural resources, protecting biodiversity, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Environmental sustainability involves promoting sustainable consumption and production, reducing waste and pollution, and creating a circular economy that promotes the reuse and recycling of resources.



In summary, sustainable development involves balancing economic growth, social development, and environmental protection to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. The three dimensions of sustainable development, economic, social, and environmental, are interconnected and must be addressed together to achieve sustainable development goals.
